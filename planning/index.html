OUR PROGRAM

Our 9-month program is divided into 12 modules.
The last module is a a 6-weeks intensive Final
Project where students work on a real project
wita real partner. Our training takes place at
Epitech (Brussels)everySunday.
During the week, students are supported online in their
self-study,homework and peer learning.

OUR IMPACT

We graduated over 100 people in Belgium and more than 300 globally. In Belgium,

OUR CORE VALUES
Community

We’re largely dependent on volunteers.
That’s what makes us an engaged grassroots
community, as our students call it ‘the HYF family’.

Diversity & inclusivenes

Our program is adapted to suit people with
different backgrounds, often in vulnerable
situations. We teach on Sundays to allow
everyone to take control of their personal
lives during the week.

Commitment
Our program is free of charge, although we
ask for strong commitment during our 7
month-journey. And you know what? Many
alumni stay actively involved!

Open
Sharing is caring. Our curriculum is 100%
open. We believe in knowledge for the many,
not the few.
